APPLE MUFFINS WITH GROUND-IVY

DIFFICULTY

Easy

COOKING TIME

60 min

PORTIONS

6

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 g wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 100 ml yoghurt
  • 500 ml flavor-neutral oil, such as grape seed
  • 100 g powdered sugar
  • 2 apples
  • A small handful of ground-ivy (or use, sweet woodruff, ground ivy, or meadow sweet)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Whisk together eggs and sugar in a bowl until white and foamy.
  2. Add wheat flour, yoghurt, oil, and baking powder to the bowl carefully folding with a rubber spatula until just incorporated. Be careful not to over-mix.
  3. Cut the apple in small cubes and chop the ground-ivy finely.
  4. Gently mix apple and ground-ivy into the dough.
  5. Divide the dough evenly into 12 greased muffin molds. Bake at 200 degrees C (400 F) for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick, inserted into a muffin, comes out clear.
  6. Let cool and sprinkle with powdered sugar. Serve warm or at room temperature.
