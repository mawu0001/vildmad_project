Welcome

Imagine a town like a big rocky playground, but with some parts covered in green like parks and gardens. Some of these green spots are like neat gardens, so you won't find tasty plants there. But if you're a little explorer looking for yummy things to eat like berries or nuts, try to find places where nature is left alone, especially away from where lots of people live. Be extra careful when foraging near busy roads or factories because there might be icky stuff around. And stay away from places where people spray things to stop plants from growing.
